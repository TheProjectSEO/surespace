Why two install numbers — clicks AND attributable installs. The first (web→App Store clicks) is something we control 100% via web traffic + landing-page CTAs. The second (attributable installs) depends on the first × the click→install rate set by App Store / Play Store flows (~50% industry standard). Both numbers are countable in Branch.io. We commit to both so it's clear where the line of control is.
What "install" means here. "Attributable app install" = a Firebase first_open event tagged via Branch.io with a web source — i.e., we can prove this person came from sure-space.com → tapped a smart link → installed → opened the app. The metric stops at first_open. Anything after that point is in your app, not on us.

Section 3 · Scope

Three columns. No ambiguity.

What we DO inside the AED 9,500 retainer · what we RECOMMEND your team execute · what's OUT of our scope entirely.

The scope line, stated plainly. Our work is on the marketing website (sure-space.com) only — SEO, content, AEO, schema, attribution. We are not building a web product or web app. All user accounts, signups, profiles, messaging, and in-app behaviour live on iOS and Android — that stays entirely with your team. The website's only job is to drive qualified traffic to the App Store / Play Store install pages.
What we DO

Marketing website only · bundled at retainer

  • Attribution build: Branch.io smart links + UTM-tagged store buttons so web→install is countable for the first time
  • Strategy: 9-cluster topical ontology · biweekly internal review
  • Content: 8-10 pages/mo · editorial + programmatic + listicles
  • Schema: MobileApplication · FAQPage · Organization · Article · Person · Event · BreadcrumbList
  • AEO: Citation capsules · LLM monitoring · entity content
  • Link building: 3-5 DR 40+ authority placements/mo (UAE prioritised where defensible, but not exclusive — we go where the relevance and authority are)
  • Cleanup: Zombie URL audit (Bangkok pages flagged) · 404 repairs · canonical fixes
  • Web SEO dev: CWV · schema deployment · CTA placement
  • Cadence: Live dashboard always on · weekly calls Month 1 only · biweekly calls Month 2+ · monthly written status · gate reviews at M4 / M7 / M11
  • Competitor tracking: Timeleft · Platinumlist · Meetup · InterNations
What we RECOMMEND

Your team executes · we advise

  • Onboarding flow tweaks based on landing-page intent data
  • Push notification cadence and content suggestions
  • Email lifecycle automation for re-engagement
  • Wingman launch — landing page + keyword coverage as the feature ships
  • Paywall placement + trigger experimentation
  • Profile completeness + verification UX nudges
  • Event RSVP → attendance reminder flow
What's OUT

Not our scope · not our metric

  • In-app product changes · matching algorithm
  • Push notifications + email lifecycle automation
  • Paywall placement, pricing, conversion UX
  • Event programming + on-day execution
  • Paid media buying · creative production
  • Customer support · community moderation
  • App Store / Play Store organic discovery
Why this delineation matters. This proposal puts us on the hook only for outputs we control end-to-end: clicks, installs from those clicks, rankings, AEO citations, listicle placements, DR. Everything in the RECOMMEND column we'll advise on; your team executes inside their tooling. Everything in the OUT column is fully outside our scope.
